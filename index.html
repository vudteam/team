Our Timeline

  • Teaming Up

    April, 2018

    We first met on our Facebook and found we had a common interest which is information security and hence we decided to team up and help each others growth by solving common issues, discussion, and learning interactively from teaching. Our team operating as the only purple team from India.

  • First Project Release

    15 June, 2018

    We released the first ever project CMSeek and it was a massive success with 250+ stars on GitHub. The standalone project now stands out as a CMS Detection and Exploitation Suite with capable module structure. Since then 7 releases have already been made and is presently under active development.

  • Another Feather To The Crown

    31 July, 2018

    We released our second ingeniuous project UBoat, a proof of concept Botnet project designated to replicate a fully weaponised commercial botnet. And not to mention, this project too was a grand success.

  • Further Growth

    At Present

    We are presently an expanding team of geeks with an aim to make this world relatively a better cyber crime free place to live in. Being the only team in India operating as a purple team, we will build defenses against various Red Teams and yet also release several offensive Red team projects to keep security relevant.
